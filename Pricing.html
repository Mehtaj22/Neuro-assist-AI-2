Plan Guide

What Should I Choose?

Starter is best for:

  • Teams moving beyond free tools to run consistent email campaigns
  • Businesses with an existing audience and basic engagement needs
  • Marketers who want simple reporting without added complexity

Upgrade to Starter when:

  • Free tools no longer meet your email or branding needs
  • You need basic performance tracking and reliability
  • You want a more professional marketing foundation

Professional is best for:

  • Teams actively generating demand across multiple channels
  • Marketers who need automation to qualify and nurture leads
  • Sales teams that rely on marketing to create pipeline

Upgrade to Professional when:

  • You want to know where new contacts are coming from
  • You need automation to route and qualify leads
  • You’re asked to show how marketing contributes to sales

Enterprise is best for:

  • Organizations running complex, data-driven customer journeys
  • Teams that need governance, permissions, and advanced analytics
  • Businesses where marketing ROI is business-critical

Upgrade to Enterprise when:

  • You need multi-touch revenue attribution
  • You manage multiple teams, brands, or regions
  • Executives or investors expect clear ROI reporting
