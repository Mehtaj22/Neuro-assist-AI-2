Plan Guide

What Should I Choose?

🌟 Starter is best for:

  • Students with basic study requirements
  • Individuals exploring AI-powered summarization for the first time
  • Casual readers and learners

Upgrade to Starter when:

  • You are new to NeuroAssist AI
  • You only need quick summaries of notes or articles
  • You want to explore AI features without advanced requirements

🚀 Professional is best for:

  • College and university students
  • Researchers and educators
  • Working professionals

Upgrade to Professional when:

  • You upload documents frequently
  • You require detailed summaries and exam notes
  • You work with large PDFs or research papers

🏢 Enterprise is best for:

  • Organizations running complex, data-driven customer journeys
  • Teams that need governance, permissions, and advanced analytics
  • Businesses where marketing ROI is business-critical

Upgrade to Enterprise when:

  • Your organization has multiple users sharing the platform
  • You process large volumes of documents every day
  • You require enterprise-grade security and scalability
